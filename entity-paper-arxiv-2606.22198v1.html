The semiconductor industry is rapidly transitioning from monolithic systems-on-chip toward heterogeneous, multi-vendor 2.5D chiplet ecosystems integrated via silicon interposers. While this paradigm shift offers immense benefits in yield, cost, and time-to-market, it radically expands the attack surface.
